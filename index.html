Chisco is a Computer Science; Games Engineering student, a part-time hipster, home cook/pizza expert, he has been described by some (mostly himself) not only as a frustrated artist, but as a frustrated: philosopher, mathematician and screenwriter. The truth is he dabbles in quite a handful of different activities in his free time.

(Which of course is just a rather wordy and pretencious way of saying: "He has different hobbies and interests". Then again being wordy and pretencious is part of the whole "ooh so misterious" vibe he's trying to achieve with the design of this Website, so yeah whatevs)

More recently he embarked on the quest of creating this very blog. He intends to update it on a somewhat regular basis, to share some of his thoughts, the projects he's working on and other pseudorandom things he finds interesting. Hopefully those updates won't require him to write everything in third person all the time though. Because right now he feels it's just a matter of time before he starts making more gramatical erros. This style of writing is very counterintuitive, like for example right now he has no idea how he is supposed to finish this paragraph? Or is it a sentence? See?

He clearly has no idea of what he's doing. Well to be fair he kinda does but, he is by no means an expert. I guess that's the whole point of this blog, having access to the mind of a dude who is perhaps a bit of a Johannes Factotum but whose insights might just be what someone out there is looking for (even if it's for laughs or mere entertainment purposes) :)
